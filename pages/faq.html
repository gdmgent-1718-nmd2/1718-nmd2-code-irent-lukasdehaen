Frequently Asked Questions

How much does this service cost?

You’re paying for Lukas’ entry ticket, plus an additional 15 EUR per 30 minutes of shoulder riding.

Is there a maximum weight?

Unfortunately Lukas is no superhero, therefore we have to limit bodyweight at 75Kg.

Is Big Friendly Shoulders suitable for people with a disability?

Lukas shoulders are suitable for people with a disability. However a regular sense of balance is required. If this isn’t the case we strongly advise you to not for participate in our services.

Are there any risks I should keep in mind?

Although Lukas will assist you to the best of his abilities, we can not rule out drops from a small height completely. We strongly advice people suffering from fear of heights and/or people with balance issues to think twice about their purchase. We do not offer refunds.

Can I book Lukas for multiple festivals?

Yes, you can book Lukas for multiple festivals. Check the agenda for more details.

Is it possible to meet Lukas beforehand?

Big Friendly Shoulders is an organisation that relies heavily on trust and human connection. Therefore, if you feel uncomfortable diving in this journey headfirst, we are happy to arange free phone conversations and meetings for a small fee, upon request.

Will Lukas be able to keep me up during a whole artist performance?

Unfortunately, Lukas does not possess that skill. Lukas will however offer 30 minute sessions with brief 5-10 minute intervals.
